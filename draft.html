In Chaos Draft, one exciting twist on the traditional format is drafting by set blocks—grouping booster packs by their original sets or themed blocks, such as Ravnica, Zendikar, or Innistrad. Instead of using completely random packs from across Magic’s history, players can draft from a curated selection within a block, preserving some synergy while still embracing the unpredictability of Chaos Draft. For example, including one pack each from Ravnica: City of Guilds, Guildpact, and Dissension offers a semi-cohesive draft environment filled with multicolor mechanics, while still giving players a taste of variety and surprise. This approach strikes a balance between chaos and cohesion, appealing to players who enjoy wild interactions but still want some mechanical familiarity.
